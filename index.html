About me

My Story

I was born and raised in Ontario, Canada, to two incredible parents who both immigrated to Canada during their high school years. My parents met while studying sciences at the University of Toronto, and after graduating, they got married, moved to London, England, and returned to Canada 4 years later after having me!

I grew up in a lively household with two younger brothers and our beloved dog, Simba. My journey as a competitive dancer began when I was just 2 years old and continued until I graduated high school at 18. Dance played a significant role in shaping who I am, but over time, I realized it was more of a hobby than a lifelong career path.

As a child, I had a vivid imagination and an insatiable curiosity. I channelled my creativity into a variety of hobbies, from experimenting in the kitchen and honing homemaking skills to exploring visual and digital arts and mastering the art of public speaking.

These pursuits have helped me grow into a well-rounded, creative, and ambitious individual with a passion for learning and self-expression. Whether it’s through the arts, academia, or day-to-day life, I’m always looking for ways to push boundaries and leave my mark.
